Part 1 - ArUco Marker Tracker

Upload a video (MP4, AVI, MOV) containing an object that has an ArUco marker and moves around. The tracker draws a box around detected markers and writes an annotated video back to you.

Please ensure the video contains visible ArUco markers on moving objects; otherwise the tracker may not find anything.

Choose a predefined ArUco dictionary or let the tracker auto-detect.

A factor to enlarge (or shrink) the tracked bounding box around the marker. 1.0 is no padding.

Choose a video to get started. For best results record a close-up moving object with an ArUco marker.
