Part 3 - Multi-Template Detection & Selective Blur

Detect all templates inside the fixed scene, then obscure those regions with adjustable blur.

Adjust the sensitivity of template matching. Higher values reduce false positives but may miss faint matches.

The multiplier scales both the Gaussian kernel size and sigma before obscuring each detection.
